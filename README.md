Project Overview
The Digital Ethics Hub is a multidisciplinary space dedicated to navigating the complex intersection of Cyber Ethics, Journalism, and Digital Animation. In an era defined by rapid AI evolution and shifting digital landscapes, this project seeks to examine how we maintain integrity, truth, and human-centric values in the stories we tell and the media we create.
Core Objectives
Ethical Storytelling: Exploring the responsibility of modern journalism in the age of misinformation and deepfakes.
Visual Integrity: Investigating how computer animation and digital art can be used as tools for advocacy while respecting intellectual property and digital consent.
Proactive Defense: Promoting cybersecurity awareness through creative narratives, making complex digital safety concepts accessible to everyone.
AI & Humanity: Analyzing the ethical implications of "Agentic AI" and its impact on creative labor and social welfare.
